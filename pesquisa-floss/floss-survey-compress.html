FLOSS Survey

This survey was developed as part of a master's research to identify the main communication issues existing in Free, Libre and Open Source Software (FLOSS) environments. All data collected from this survey will be used anonymously and collective results will be publicly published.

The average time needed to finish this survey is approximately 5 minutes. The captcha system used at the end intends to diminish the amount of invalid data provided. We apologize for this inconvenient.

We received the support of the State University of Sao Paulo (USP), the Free Software Competence Center (CCSL) at IME-USP and the QualiPSo project. We would also like to thank Sourceforge.net, GitHub.com, LaunchPad.net, CodeHaus.org and Google Code for helping this survey to reach a broader audience.
