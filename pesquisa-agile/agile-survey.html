Distributed Agile Survey

This survey was developed as part of a master's research that aims to identify the main communication issues existing in Agile environments. All data collected from this survey will be used anonymously and results will be publicly published once the survey is over.

The average time needed to finish this survey is 5 minutes. The captcha system used at the end intends to reduce the amount of invalid data provided. I apologize for this inconvenience.

I received the support of the State University of Sao Paulo (USP), the Free Software Competence Center (CCSL) at IME-USP and the QualiPSo project.
